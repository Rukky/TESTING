About Ibrahim

Ibrahim Hassan Hadejia was the former deputy governor of Jigawa state from 2003-2007 and from 2015- 2019. After a rigorous campaign he emerged victorious over several formidable opponents to secure the office of the Jigawa state senator(North-East) Also known as Barrister Ibrahim, he has served as the Attorney General, Commisioner of Justice and The SSG (Secretary to the State Government) of Jigawa before running alongside Alhaji Saminu Turaki for the deputy governor's postion in 2003.
